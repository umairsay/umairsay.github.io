Reweight the Market

Visible Value argued that MLS pays for what's visible — attack, progression — and not for what isn't. Attack, creation, progression, dribbling, and defense are five composite scores built for that paper. Set how much each one counts below. Watch who the market would pay if it valued things differently.

Set the weights

Each fader controls how much one standard deviation of improvement in that area is worth in salary, holding everything else fixed. Equal weights reproduce the overall score used in Visible Value. The middle preset loads the actual coefficients estimated in the dissertation's wage regression; the third is illustrative, not estimated.
